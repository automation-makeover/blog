Jeff Hodson Appointed New First Vice President at Seattle's CBRE

CBRE Capital Markets welcomes Jeff Hodson to their ranks as a First Vice President who will be based in Seattle. His induction into the Pacific Northwest branch bolsters the leadership team that comprises of Charles Safley and Tom Pehl. The cohesive triad will bolster the team's dedication to intensifying their commercial real estate investment sales domain.

The team specializes in operating across a diversified portfolio of real estate types. These include office space, flex properties, life science-centered real estate, and land transactions. Hodson's addition will add a fresh dynamism to the team's focus on these areas.

Before his induction at CBRE, Hodson held remarkable positions in his illustrious career. Information on his career progression before joining CBRE, and the journey that saw him climb to this senior role, are available on further exploration. His wide experience will undoubtedly be utilized to add another layer of expertise to the collective skill sets of Safley and Pehl's team.

CBRE Capital Markets is excited to have Hodson on board and is confident that his addition will aid the company's commitment to offering unparalleled comprehensive commercial real estate solutions. Amidst the changing demands of today's real estate industry, CBRE's strategic hire of Hodson continues to fulfill its unwavering commitment to top-tier staffing while providing exceptional services to its clientele.
